Instructions: Connect the Dots

On the left is a set of documents describing relationships between various items (people, locations, organizations, etc.) Those same items are also shown on the right; each item is a dot on the visualization. Your task is to "connect the dots"; that is, to create links between the dots that visualize their relationships in the documents.

You can pick an item in any of 3 ways:

  1. click the item in the document
  2. click the dot in the visualization, or
  3. pick the item from the menu above the visualization.

Next, pick the item you want to link it to. Finally, describe the link in a few words using the "relationship" box, and click "Create Link".
